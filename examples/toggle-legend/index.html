Toggling series from the legend

The toggleLegend plugin allows you to toggle the visibility of series on the chart from the legend. It also provides a mechanism for showing which series are visible in the legend.

Click on a series in the legend to toggle it on or off.

The axes always retain their original scale when series are hidden, so as not to make the display of data more confusing.
